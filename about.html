About Vidurasa

Vidurasa Agricultural Products is a premier supplier of authentic Ceylon Cinnamon and Black Pepper, based in the heart of Sri Lanka.

With a deep-rooted passion for agriculture and a commitment to quality, we bridge the gap between local Sri Lankan farmers and international markets. Our mission is to provide the world with the finest spices while supporting sustainable farming practices in our local communities.

"True Cinnamon" (Cinnamomum verum) is native to Sri Lanka, and we take pride in exporting this exquisite spice in its purest form. Whether you are looking for Alba, C5, or C4 grade sticks, or high-quality powder, we ensure that every shipment meets rigorous quality standards.
