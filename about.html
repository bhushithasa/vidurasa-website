Ceylon Cinnamon: The "True" Cinnamon

Ceylon cinnamon, or "true cinnamon," is distinct from Cassia (Cinnamomum cassia), the more common variety, with its delicate, sweet flavor and significantly lower coumarin content. Harvested exclusively from the inner bark of the Cinnamomum verum tree in Sri Lanka, it is prized for its subtle aroma, smoother texture, and numerous health benefits.

Physical Characteristics

  • ✨ Delicate, mildly sweet
  • 🌿 Aromatic
  • 📜 Many layers of bark
  • 🏷️ Pale brown color

Low Coumarin* Levels (0.004%)

*Coumarin is a natural compound present in higher amounts in Cassia Cinnamon (1%) compared to Ceylon Cinnamon (0.004%). High doses of coumarin can be harmful, making true Ceylon cinnamon the much safer choice for regular consumption.
