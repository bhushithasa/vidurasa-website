About Vidurasa

Vidurasa Agricultural Products is your dedicated export partner for authentic Ceylon Cinnamon and Black Pepper, operating directly from the heart of Sri Lanka.

We bridge the critical gap between local Sri Lankan estates and the global bulk spice market. Our mission is to provide international wholesale buyers, food manufacturers, and distributors with a secure, consistent, and fully traceable supply chain of the world's finest spices.

As specialists in Cinnamomum verum (True Cinnamon), we take pride in exporting this luxury spice in its purest form. Whether you are procuring premium Alba or C5 sticks for high-end retail packaging, or require precision-milled powder for industrial beverage manufacturing, we ensure every shipment meets rigorous international food safety and quality standards.

Wholesale Export Products

Ceylon Cinnamon Sticks

Premium export-grade Cinnamomum verum. Hand-rolled, boasting consistent grading (Alba, C5, C4) and rigorous moisture-controlled processing to guarantee quality for importers and global distributors.

  • Alba (6mm): Ultra-premium grade, perfect for luxury retail and gourmet culinary markets.
  • C5 (10-12mm): Smooth & visually appealing, standard premium grade for retail packaging.
  • C4 (13-14mm): Excellent sweet flavor profile, highly efficient for commercial food service.

Cinnamon Powder

100% pure Ceylon cinnamon bark, finely milled to your specified mesh size under strict temperature control to preserve essential volatile oils. Ideal for commercial bakeries, beverage manufacturing, and wholesale spice distributors.

Cinnamon Quillings

High-quality broken pieces collected during the baling process. Delivers the exact same signature flavor and aroma profile as whole sticks at a lower cost. The preferred choice for essential oil distillation and industrial spice grinding.

Cinnamon Cut Pieces (TBC)

Machine-cut to precise, uniform lengths (Tea Bag Cut). Clean, dust-free, and size-consistent across bulk export orders. Perfect for commercial tea blending, ready-to-drink (RTD) beverage manufacturing, and culinary spice blends.

Sri Lankan Black Pepper

Premium dark berries naturally sun-dried to optimal export moisture levels. Renowned globally for higher piperine content and intense aroma. Available in bulk volumes for international meat processors, food manufacturers, and retail brands.
